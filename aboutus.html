WHO ARE WE?

OUR VISION

Our vision as Decodians is beyond just decoding our daily routines and semester exams. Our vision is to decode all life's major and minor problems. The foundation stone of the Exam Decode was laid on the hope that ten ordinary students would do something that no one had ever done before, would do something stunning. Whether coding, sketching, VFX, GFX, management, marketing, or gaming, each member of ED is gifted with a special talent, and we aspire to be the world's leading face in these streams. Humanity is an eminent part of us; earning seven figures is not the end goal of life. Adding value to one's life lies at the zenith of our priority. Friendship and companionship matter to us, we may possess contrasting traits from each other, but it is our differences that bind us together more potent than ever. And lastly, we're not the Decodians because we decode problems, but the Decodians because we decode them together.

OUR PROMISES

As Decodians, we desire to aspire before we expire. We want to assist each other in our lives with endless possibilities and be the best of ourselves, so our parents can be proud of us. Our goals aren't just to grab a decent package and then live the rest of our lives as an employee, as Decodians want to leave a mark as the footprints on the sand of times so that someone else who passes by the deserts shall know and be inspired by the fact that anything is possible. Apart from boosting the whole world, our primary aspiration is to be even better friends in the future as compared to what we are now. We aspire to understand each other on a much better scale and try our best to be there for each other in the most extreme circumstances. We don't know the future, but we can't imagine a future world without Exam Decode. Friendship and Exam Decode sound synonymous to us and we promise it shall sound the same in the future.

OUR JOURNEY

Just like a ten-string guitar where all the strings have different tunes, ten people all belonging to different backgrounds came together. They blended in so well as the harmonious melody of a Rabindra Sangeet as if they were part of one big family. Each of us, coming from a different origin with a different ideology, was united under the unconditional bond of friendship. No matter the situation, we always had each other's back through all the ups and downs. It's not that our journey went smooth throughout; it was more of a thrilling roller coaster ride with its local maxima and minima. No matter the situation, we always find a way to invest a better part of ourselves into understanding each other instead of fighting. We always tried to be integrated as a family because friendship is a family. What makes us so close isn't the time we spend together having an outing but rather the time we spend together in college classes. All of us live so far apart that it is unfeasible to have an outing outside the college, but isn't college good enough? Most of the memories we created and will create in the Future won't be as much in a Trip but inside the four walls of a classroom. What differentiates us from the others is that we're open to anyone joining us and spending time with us, whether on outings or sharing our classroom seats. One of the major factors that also sets us apart from the rest is the mere fact that we represent such diverse integrity. Each one of us represents ourselves in a manner that is just too different from each other; for instance, and one defines how fancy a life can be, one can teach how to face problems, one supports you in every aspect. One can create the most beautiful designs; one just paints the entire universe on a fine canvas holding a brush, and one can be an eSport T1 player. One pleases others with his witty jokes, one loves to do abstract things, one can code an entire webpage or an app, and we all know that friendship is the most divine bond in all of mankind.

HERE WE ARE!
